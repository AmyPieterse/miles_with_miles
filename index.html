    Meet Your Guide

    Hello, I'm Miles

    Welcome to my world! I started Touring Miles with Miles because I believe travel should be more than just sightseeing – it should be about creating connections, sharing stories, and making memories that last a lifetime.

    Born and raised in Cape Town, I've spent over a decade exploring every hidden gem, secret viewpoint, and local treasure this incredible region has to offer. My passion is sharing the authentic Cape Town experience – from the bustling V&A Waterfront to the serene wine lands, from the dramatic coastlines of Hermanus to the lush forests of the Garden Route.

    My philosophy is simple: by the end of our journey together, you won't just have seen South Africa – you'll have felt its heartbeat, tasted its soul, and become part of our extended family.

    Your Day-by-Day Adventure

    Experience every moment of your South African journey with our carefully planned daily itineraries

    Cape Town Package
    10-Day Cape Town Experience

    Including Aquila Game Reserve Safari & Salt Boutique Hotel

    1
    Arrival & City Orientation

    Airport pickup, check-in at Salt Boutique Hotel, welcome dinner, and Cape Town city orientation tour

    2
    Table Mountain & V&A Waterfront

    Cable car to Table Mountain, explore V&A Waterfront, visit Two Oceans Aquarium, sunset dinner

    3
    Cape Peninsula Tour

    Chapman's Peak, Cape Point, penguin colony at Boulders Beach, traditional South African braai

    4
    Winelands Adventure

    Stellenbosch wine tasting, historic Franschhoek, cellar tours, gourmet lunch with wine pairings

    5
    Aquila Game Reserve Safari

    Full-day Big Five safari at Aquila Game Reserve, game drives, wildlife photography, traditional potjiekos lunch

    6
    Robben Island & Bo-Kaap

    Robben Island historical tour, colorful Bo-Kaap district, Cape Malay cooking class, spice route exploration

    7
    Hermanus Whale Watching

    Hermanus whale watching (seasonal), coastal walk, cliff path exploration, seafood lunch overlooking the ocean

    8
    Lion's Head & Camps Bay

    Lion's Head hike (sunrise), Camps Bay beach relaxation, luxury spa experience, rooftop dinner

    9
    Cultural Immersion

    Township tour, local community visit, traditional music and dance, craft market shopping, farewell dinner

    10
    Departure

    Final breakfast, last-minute shopping at V&A Waterfront, airport transfer with memories to last a lifetime
